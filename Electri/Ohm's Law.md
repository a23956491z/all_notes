#circuit_analysis

$i$是電流， $R$是電阻
電位差 $v$，$v(t) = i(t)R$  

電功率 $p$，$p(t) = v(t)i(t)$  

電導（electrical conductance）$G$：傳輸電流的強弱
$G = 1/R$，單位是 Siemens 縮寫 S


分壓  
$v_{R_1} = \frac{R_1}{R_1 + R_2}v(t)$，$v_{R_2} = \frac{R_2}{R_1 + R_2}v(t)$
![](https://i.imgur.com/sjdW7zA.png)

  
  
分流  
$i_1(t) = \frac{R_2}{R_1 + R_2}i(t)$，$i_2(t) = \frac{R_1}{R_1 + R_2}i(t)$
